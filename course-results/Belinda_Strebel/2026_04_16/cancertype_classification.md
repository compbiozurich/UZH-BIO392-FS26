### CDKN2A gene CNV query 
CNV-related query shown is a gene-level CNV frequency or focal deletion map: 
- CDKN2A is located on chromosome 9p21.3
- it shows the frequency of focal deletions at that locus across samples 
- results are shown in a histogram/ frequency plot highliting how often that genomic region is lost/ gained across the samples in the database

CDKN2A encodes 2 tumor suppressors (p16/INK4A and p14/ARF) and homozygous 
deletion at 9p21 is one of the most common CNV events across many cancer types,
including mesothelioma (~70-80% of cases).
