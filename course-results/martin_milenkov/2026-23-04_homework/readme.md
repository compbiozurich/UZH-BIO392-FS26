## • Uniprot ID, protein N me, length, function
- P06865 · HEXA_HUMAN, Beta-hexosaminidase subunit alpha, 529 aa long
- Hydrolyzes the non-reducing end N-acetyl-D-hexosamine and/or sulfated N-acetyl-D-hexosamine of glycoconjugates, such as the oligosaccharide moieties from proteins and neutral glycolipids, or from certain mucopolysaccharides.

## • Which mutations & diseases?
- Mutation variant NP_001305754.1:p.Glu493Ter with deletion of E and premature termination at position 493, leading to nonfunctional protein and development of Tay-Sachs disease

## • Sub-cellular location?
- in the lysosome

- Disulfide bond between Cys58 and Cys-104, Cys-277 and Cys-328, and Cys-505 and Cys-522
